About Me

2nd-year master's student at Nagoya University, Japan.
Supervisor: Nobuo Kawaguchi and Takuro Yonezawa

Hello, I'm Haru Terashima, Graduate School of Engineering at Nagoya University(Japan). I'm a member of Kawaguchi Laboratory(UCLab). I received my B.E degrees in engineering from Nagoya University, Japan in 2024. My research interests are Urban Computing(Human Behavior Modeling, Human Mobility Prediction) and Machine Learning(Transformer, LLM).
My hobbies are playing darts, solving a mystery, running, and traveling.
